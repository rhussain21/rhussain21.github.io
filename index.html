About Me

Hi there, I'm Redwan!

I am a data science graduate student and former electrical engineer with 8+ years of technical and project management experience. My expertise lies in developing data analytics tools that merge computer science, modeling, and statistics principles.

My fascination with data science began during my leadership of a utility EV charging program. Limited data availability for EVs motivated me to run pilot programs and explore advanced data analysis, predictive modeling, and actionable insights.

Transitioning into the manufacturing sector further fueled my passion as I witnessed the impact of implementing analytics tools to optimize machine data, effectively managing downtime and operational costs.

My unique background in traditional engineering drives my commitment to utilizing data science techniques to solve real-world challenges.
